Mr. Mahendra Choudhary
Mahendra Choudhary is the owner of Sai Shraddha General Store (SSGS), where he has successfully managed and operated the business for over 15 years. His extensive experience in retail has allowed him to build a reputation for offering a wide range of quality products, including everyday household items, groceries, and general merchandise. Mahendra's dedication to customer service and attention to detail have helped him maintain a loyal customer base and ensure the store's continued success. His long-standing experience in the business reflects his commitment and adaptability in the competitive retail market.
